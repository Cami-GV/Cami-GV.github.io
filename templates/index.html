I am a master student in Departamento de Matemáticas at Universidad de Chile, I am working with professor Anita Rojas on Geometry of Riemann surfaces . Particularly, I am focused on algorithms to find systoles of surfaces of genus 2 and 3, in order to describe those surfaces with the longest systoles and biggest kissing number.
