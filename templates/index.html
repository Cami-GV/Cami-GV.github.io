I am a master student in Departamento de Matemáticas at Universidad de Chile, I am working with professor Anita Rojas in Geometry of Riemann surfaces . In my thesis I am trying to find those surfaces of genus 2 and 3 with the longest systoles and biggest kissing number. Particularly, I am focused on to describe algorithms to determine systoles starting with an action of an abstract group.
